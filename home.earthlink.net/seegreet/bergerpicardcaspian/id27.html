A wonderful marriage between two positively STUNNING Champions! Ch Aquina Vallee de los Volcanos aka "Sugar" is a beautiful example of everything feminine in a Berger Picard! She is very elegant and has an amazing temperament which is how she earned her call name "Sugar"! She also earned an "Excellent" in her conformation and movement evaluation by the prestigious Mr. JC Larive - president of the French Berger Picard Club - Les Amis du Berger Picard. "Sugar": is also a very diligent and devoted mother! This will be her last litter. Some additional information below about this positively beautiful girl!
