Like her namesake, Ch Allstars Elizabeth Taylor is a positively lovely young lady with a lot of spunk! She is a wonderful mother and this will be her second litter. She has a wonderful temperament, has had all her health clearances and has beautiful conformation with a rating of "Excellent" by Mr. Larive from the 2010 American Élevage.
