All the Latest Training News

2013 - Cassie and I have been busy learning and practicing the art of conformation this year both in the ring and in the classroom and have succeeded admirably as we now have the only title currently available in the AKC for Berger Picards, the 'Certificate of Merit'! We are currently and for next year will be focusing more on the sport of Agility as we have a date with our good friend Christina Potter and her Berger Picard, Chester Gigalo!  He has his own published book (link below) and can be seen in MasterCard, Verizon, Company Store, and many other commercials. 10% of all proceeds go to the health fund for the Berger Picard =) We have a lot of work ahead of us but can hardly wait till November 2014!

Chester Gigolo: Diary of a Dog Star

 
 2012 - Cassie will be turning five years old this year. We have graduated from our regular one on one sessions with the professional trainers. Now, all we really continue are our doggie classes, which are much more for training me than for Cassie (he is such a clever fellow)! Classes we go to include obedience, agility, and herding on a rotating basis.  We also attend conformation classes occassionally as there is always something new to learn. Having gone through all of the training thus far with Cassie has inspired me to attend dog training college, so I can learn as quick as he does! =) It is nice to know that, if anything in the future should come up, this wonderful group of trainers will always be available to help us out. And of course, there is the next Picard we are looking forward to adding to our home! =) Thank you Wendy, Shari, Ann, Kathan, Pepe, Leisl, Mark and the many others who have been such a wonderful source of inspiration, knowledge, and training!
 
This past year, 2010, Caspian and the family have been visiting the trainer(s) together so that we can all learn how to best work with Cassie. Therefore, they (the trainers'), have not had to write any notes. We hope to involve Cassie w/o family, in a new activity soon, that will have video notes for everyone.
-Celestia 2010
